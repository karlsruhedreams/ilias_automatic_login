How to redirect to a course with the ILIAS automatic login extension

In ILIAS, if you navigate to a course, but you are logged out, after the login procedure the original target URL of the course might disappear and you end up on a general ILIAS-page. To avoid this, you can let the ILIAS automatic login extension handle your redirects.

To do this, create a URI of this form: moz-extension://[extension internal UUID]/redirect.html?redirectUrl=[encoded URL of the course].

If you click the button below, an URL of the above kind is created from the URL entered into the text box below, and copied to your clipboard.
